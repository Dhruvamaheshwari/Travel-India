Munnar

Munnar in Kerala, South India, enchants with its rolling tea plantations, mist-covered hills, and tranquil landscapes.

Hampi

Hampi in Karnataka, a UNESCO World Heritage site, mesmerizes with its surreal boulder-strewn landscape, ancient ruins, and rich historical tapestry.

Ooty

Ooty in Tamil Nadu, a picturesque hill station, captivates with its lush landscapes, colonial charm, and serene ambiance.

Kochi

Kochi in Kerala, a vibrant coastal city, blends historic forts, a bustling waterfront, and a rich cultural fusion.

Goa

Goa, on India's western coast, captivates with its sun-kissed beaches, vibrant nightlife, and a charming blend of Portuguese-influenced architecture.

Tirupati

Tirupati in Andhra Pradesh is a revered pilgrimage destination, home to the sacred Tirumala Venkateswara Temple atop the Seven Hills.

Visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam, a coastal city in Andhra Pradesh, delights with its picturesque beaches, the INS Kursura Submarine Museum, and the scenic Araku Valley nearby.

Alappuzha

Alappuzha, known as the "Venice of the East," enchants with its serene backwaters, houseboat cruises, and vibrant Nehru Trophy Boat Race.
